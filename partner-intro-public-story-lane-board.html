Public story lane selector

Decide whether one approved public-safe story asset should help the original warm-intro public boundary or the referral public boundary next.

Once one story-safe asset is approved for public use, the next job is not dropping it across every page. The real job is choosing the public story lane it should strengthen first: should it help the original warm-intro public story boundary keep converting, or should it help a closed buyer introduce one more fit lead through the referral public story boundary?

Public Story Lane Rule

Choose the story lane first, then choose the narrower trust, proof, or case job inside that boundary.

One approved story asset can help more than one commercial path, but it should not do all of them at once. Good routing chooses the story lane first, keeps the hidden buyer and relationship layer locked, and prevents one public asset from drifting between original-intro and referral claims.
