Close Rule

Do not send payment early.

Payment page is for clear movement only. Before that, the job is to tighten scope, confirm the bottleneck, and let the offer page do the framing.

After Payment

Keep post-payment motion simple and explicit.

1. Confirm Receipt

Ask for the tx hash, confirm it, and acknowledge the agreed scope.

2. Collect Brief

Get the project brief and any relevant URLs so there is no delay between payment and delivery.

3. Start Sprint

Confirm the deliverables and start the short sprint immediately.
