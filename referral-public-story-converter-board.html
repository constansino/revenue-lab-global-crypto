Best Monetization Routes

Most referral public stories should point into one of these narrower next moves.

Another Referral Ask

Best when the same referral-safe story can help one more aligned introduction without rewriting the proof from scratch.

Partner Reuse

Best when the story should mostly help broader partner demand instead of staying only in the referral-specific public lane.

Case-Study Escalation

Best when the story needs one bounded public case page before it carries a larger commercial ask.

Proof To Payment

Best when the story already does enough trust work and the buyer now just needs a compressed close path.

Direct Payment Path

Best when the story already does enough selling and the buyer now just needs a clean paid start.

Do-Not-Sprawl Route

Best when one public story is already enough and the next gain comes from routing better, not publishing more pages.
