Same-Day Rule

Do not confuse a ready buyer with more process than they need.

Same-day closes work when scope is already tight, trust is already high enough, and the next useful action is payment. The mistake is adding extra pages, calls, or optional branches after the buyer has already decided.

Best Same-Day Signals

Use this page only when the buyer is actually close.

Asks For Payment

If they ask how to pay or how to get started, they are already beyond the proof stage.

Accepts The Scope

If they are no longer debating the bottleneck or the package, the deal is ready for compression.

Wants Speed

If they explicitly want to move today, do not slow them down with extra optional reading.

Short Same-Day Lines
