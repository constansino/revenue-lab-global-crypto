Partner Public Follow-Up Rule

Good follow-up removes friction. It does not reopen the entire close.

Once agreement exists, the follow-up should stay narrow. Confirm the payer, confirm the route, confirm the timing, and confirm what happens after the transfer. If a blocker remains, route to the exact page that solves it.
