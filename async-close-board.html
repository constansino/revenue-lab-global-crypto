No-call close path

Close in writing when a call would only slow the deal down.

This page is for buyers who are serious but do not want another scheduling step. If the scope is mostly clear, confidence is high enough, and the next useful move is written confirmation plus payment, keep the deal asynchronous.

Async Rule

Written clarity beats a live call when the real blocker is already small.

Calls are useful when the scope is fuzzy or trust is low. They are wasteful when the buyer mainly needs one clean written summary, one proof asset if needed, and a direct deposit path.

Short Async Lines

Scope Line

Proof Line

Deposit Line
