Decision:
- Do not try to out-platform n8n or Activepieces first.
- Do not keep shipping generic template boards as the main monetization thesis.
- Best wedge right now is a narrow document workflow product:
  `DocSafe Flow`
- Positioning:
  secure client document intake + redaction + approval workspace

Why this wedge:
- It sits inside a clearly paid market with strong existing proof.
- It is much smaller than building a full PDF platform.
- It is easier to sell than a broad automation platform.
- It supports setup fees, monthly subscriptions, white-labeling, and self-hosting.

Target buyers:
- agencies
- legal / signing teams
- consulting delivery teams
- recruiting / outsourcing teams
- any small team processing 30-300 client files per month with messy manual workflows
