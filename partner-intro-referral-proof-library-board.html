Asset Types

These are the referred proof assets that usually travel farthest without widening the story.

Anonymous Result Card

One buyer-hidden line on the account type, the bottleneck, and the visible shift.

Brandless Before-After

One stripped proof unit that shows movement without logos, screenshots, or traceable detail.

Quote-Led Slot

One approved sentence plus one light context line when voice carries more trust than story volume.

Redacted Evidence Tile

One masked detail when a concrete artifact matters more than a plain summary but raw proof would reveal too much.

Repeat-Lane Unit

One proof asset designed to help the same referral lane repeat without becoming a broader public claim.

Do-Not-Widen Asset

A proof unit that stays valuable because it remains smaller than a public case page or site-wide proof strip.
