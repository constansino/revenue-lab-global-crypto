Public Trust Family Rule

Choose the trust family first, then choose the narrower trust page, router, or proof escalation inside that family.

One approved trust asset can help more than one commercial path, but it should not do all of them at once. Good routing chooses the trust family first, keeps the hidden buyer and relationship path locked, and prevents the same asset from drifting between original-intro and referral trust claims.
