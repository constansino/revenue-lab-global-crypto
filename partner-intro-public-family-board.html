Public Family Rule

Choose the public family first, then choose the narrower lane, story family, or router inside that family.

One approved public asset can help more than one commercial path, but it should not do all of them at once. Good routing chooses the public family first, keeps the hidden relationship path locked, and prevents the same asset from drifting between original-intro and referral claims.
