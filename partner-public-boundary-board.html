Partner public reuse boundary

Before one approved public-safe partner asset spreads, freeze the public boundary that keeps the lane safe.

One approved partner-safe asset can be commercially useful across a page, a live board, a proof block, or a payment route, but that does not mean it should spread everywhere by default. This page helps lock what the asset may repeat, what must stay single-use, what stays hidden, and when the next move should stay inside the same partner public system or shift deliberately.

Where Boundary Pays

Most approved partner public assets should widen only into one of these surfaces first.

Exact Public Page

Best when the asset still needs one page shell before it becomes a wider live public unit.

Live Public Board

Best when the asset can already live as one operating public unit with one promise and one next route.

Compact Proof Block

Narrow Revenue Route
