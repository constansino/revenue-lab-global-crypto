Referral Case Family Rule

Keep the story in the referral public case family unless the public narrative job now clearly belongs to the broader warm-intro case system.

Referral-safe case can travel farther without becoming generic. Good routing chooses one case family first, keeps buyer and referrer identity locked, and stops one approved asset from drifting between referral and original-intro claims without a deliberate reason.
