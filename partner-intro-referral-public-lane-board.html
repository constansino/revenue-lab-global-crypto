Referral Public Lane Rule

Keep the asset in the referral public lane unless the public job now clearly belongs to the broader warm-intro lane.

Referral-safe public assets can travel farther without becoming generic. Good routing chooses one lane first, keeps buyer and referrer identity locked, and stops one approved asset from drifting between referral and original-intro claims without a deliberate reason.
