Referral Family Rule

Keep the asset in the referral family unless the commercial job now clearly belongs to the broader partner family.

Referral-safe assets can travel farther without becoming fully generic. Good routing chooses one family first, keeps buyer and referrer context locked, and stops one asset from drifting between referral and partner claims without a deliberate reason.
