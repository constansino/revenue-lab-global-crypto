Router Rule

Do not send the same link to every buyer.

Routing works when the first link already feels native to the buyer's context. Match the niche and the bottleneck first, then move them deeper into proof, pricing, or payment.

Simple Routes

Use short routing logic in live conversations.

If They Need Fit

Route them to the niche page that sounds most like their business before talking about pricing.

If They Need Confidence

Route them to proof or trust pages before trying to push them toward payment.

If They Need A Smaller Step

Route them to the audit page instead of forcing a full sprint too early.

Good routing lowers explanation time and improves conversion quality.
