Referral Story Lane Rule

Keep the story in the referral public boundary unless the public narrative job now clearly belongs to the broader warm-intro boundary.

Referral-safe public stories can travel farther without becoming generic. Good routing chooses one story lane first, keeps buyer and referrer identity locked, and stops one approved story from drifting between referral and original-intro claims without a deliberate reason.
