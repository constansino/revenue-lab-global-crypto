Best Destinations

Most widened warm-intro case assets belong in one of these six destinations.

Public Case-Study Page

Best when the asset still needs one narrow public story page instead of an immediate commercial handoff.

Case-Study Converter

Best when the public page is already good enough and now needs one clear revenue route instead of more visibility.

Public Proof Page

Best when the story can shrink into one bounded proof block without needing a fuller public page.

Public Trust Page

Best when the main job is lightweight credibility rather than a broader before-after narrative.

Repeat Or Expansion Route

Best when the asset should mainly create the next intro, repeat lane, or broader commercial follow-through.

Stay Smaller Instead

Best when broader public placement adds more exposure than leverage and the case still works as a tighter proof asset.
