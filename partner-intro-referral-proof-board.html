Referral Proof Rule

Referral proof works best when the trust layer is narrow, relevant, and safe for the buyer and referrer boundary.

The referred buyer usually does not need more proof. They need the right proof: one result line, one before-after shift, or one masked angle that fits the first paid step under discussion and does not expose the hidden path behind the referral.
