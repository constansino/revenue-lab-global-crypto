Best Monetization Routes

Most referral public case pages should point into one of these narrower next moves.

Another Referral Ask

Best when the same referral-safe story can help one more aligned introduction without rewriting the proof.

Partner Reuse

Best when the case should mostly help broader partner demand instead of staying only in the referral-specific public lane.

Renewal Step

Best when the same buyer now has one fresh bottleneck that deserves another paid cycle rather than more public packaging.

Retainer Stability

Best when the system now creates a repeating monthly need rather than another isolated sprint.

Direct Payment Path

Best when the page already does enough selling and the buyer now just needs a clean paid start.

Do-Not-Sprawl Route

Best when one public case page is already enough and the next gain comes from routing better, not publishing more story.
