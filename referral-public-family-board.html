Referral Public Family Rule

Keep the asset in the referral public family unless the public job now clearly belongs to the broader partner family.

Referral-safe public assets can travel farther without becoming generic. Good routing chooses one family first, keeps buyer and referrer context locked, and stops one asset from drifting between referral and partner claims without a deliberate reason.
