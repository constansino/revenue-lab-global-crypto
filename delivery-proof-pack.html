Pack Rule

Summarize what changed, not every task performed.

The best delivery proof pack is concise. It names the starting leak, the shipped assets, the visible shift, and the next clean action for approval or reuse.

Pack Shape

Every delivery proof pack should answer these four questions.

1. What Was The Leak?

State the original bottleneck in plain language so the result has a clear reference point.

2. What Shipped?

List the delivered assets only: the flow, the structure, the SOP, the page, or the handoff component that was actually completed.

3. What Changed?

Explain the visible shift: clearer next step, faster response path, stronger confidence, or cleaner owner handoff.

4. What Can Be Reused?

Note what is safe to turn into proof, case-study framing, or referral support after approval.
