Evidence Rule

Signed documents only stay credible when visible marks, timestamps, signer metadata, cryptographic standard, verification output, and archive proof all agree.

Public repo signals keep pointing to the same proof pressure: buyers want visible signatures to actually render on final PDFs, timestamps that survive validation, signer IP and date metadata in the evidence layer, stronger signature standards like PAdES or eIDAS-aware proof, and a verifiable artifact that legal or compliance teams can review later.
