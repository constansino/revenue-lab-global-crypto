Client-referral proof rights page

Fix the reuse boundary before one referral win quietly turns into a broader public claim.

Referral proof becomes risky at the exact moment it becomes useful. A closed first step created a result line, a quote, or a compact proof pack, and now people want to reuse it in more places. This page freezes the boundary first: what stays private, what can help future referral packets, what can support repeat work, and what still cannot travel publicly without a fresh explicit decision.

Reuse Boundary Rule

Approval of a referral result is not the same thing as approval of future proof reuse.

Every referral proof asset should answer four things clearly: what the asset is, where it can safely travel now, what remains off-limits, and when the boundary gets reviewed again.

Reuse Classes

Most referral proof should sit inside one of these tighter classes.

Private Only

The asset helps the current owner and buyer, but it should not travel beyond this closed referral cycle.

Referral-Pack Safe

The asset can support a future referral packet if names, screenshots, and hidden economics stay out.

Repeat-Lane Safe

The asset can help the next similar buyer without becoming a broad public claim or website proof block.

Renewal Review Needed

The asset may help a second sprint or retainer discussion, but the boundary should be reviewed again for the new paid step.

Not Public Yet

The result may be strong, but it still is not cleared for open public proof or wider case-study packaging.

Short Lines

Use short language that keeps referral proof rights narrow and explicit.

Boundary Line

This result is useful, but I want to keep the referral reuse boundary explicit before it travels any further.

Rights Line

Destination Line

Review Line

If we want to use this again for renewal, expansion, or a new lane, we should review the boundary again at that point.
