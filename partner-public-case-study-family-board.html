Public Case Family Rule

Choose the case family first, then choose the narrower case page, router, or converter inside that family.

One approved case asset can help more than one commercial path, but it should not do all of them at once. Good routing chooses the case family first, keeps the hidden buyer and channel path fixed, and prevents the same case asset from drifting between partner-public and referral-public claims.
