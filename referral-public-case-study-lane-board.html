Referral Case Lane Rule

Keep the story in the referral public case lane unless the public narrative job now clearly belongs to the broader partner-public case system.

Referral-safe case assets can travel farther without becoming generic. Good routing chooses one case lane first, keeps buyer and referrer identity locked, and stops one approved case asset from drifting between referral-public and partner-public claims without a deliberate reason.
