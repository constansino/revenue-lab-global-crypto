Warm-intro client referral page

Ask a satisfied warm-intro buyer for one relevant introduction, not a vague promise to “send people later.”

After one warm-intro win is closed and public-safe proof exists, the current buyer may be able to introduce someone else with the same bottleneck. This page keeps that ask narrow: one buyer pattern, one public-safe proof asset, one clear forward link, and one obvious route for the referred buyer if they reply.

Referral Rule

A warm-intro referral ask works best when the buyer can picture one other fit and forward one safe asset fast.

The ask should answer five things fast: who the next fit is, which proof page is safe to send, what stays hidden, what the buyer should say, and where the new lead goes next.
