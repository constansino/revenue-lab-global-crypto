Revision Rule

Keep revisions light so the sprint stays fast.

What Revisions Are For

Revisions are for tightening the delivered sprint assets, not for changing the core problem or reopening scope from zero.

What Changes Are Fine

Tone changes, CTA tightening, wording adjustments, and small structural clarifications are within normal revision logic.

What Is New Scope

Adding brand-new flows, unrelated pages, or a second bottleneck is a new scope decision, not a revision.

Completion

Define done clearly so approval is easy.

Done Means

The agreed sprint assets are delivered, one light revision cycle is handled if needed, and the owner has the handoff material.

Success Metric

Faster first response time, fewer missed quote opportunities, and cleaner intake detail for the person who closes.

Commercial Close

Deposit starts the sprint. Remaining balance is due on delivery.

Flow

Brief first. Delivery second. Handoff third.
