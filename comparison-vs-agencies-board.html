Comparison Rule

The point is not “better at everything.” The point is “better for this buying situation.”

Buyers do not need a theory lecture. They need to understand why this model is the cleaner fit when they want one commercial bottleneck fixed quickly.

Comparison Map

Use the contrast that matters most to the buyer.

Vs Agency

Agencies often come with broader process, more layers, and slower movement. The sprint is narrower and designed for one fast, commercial fix.

Vs Freelancer

Freelance work can become open-ended task execution. The sprint stays fixed around one bottleneck and one delivery boundary.

Vs Consultant

Consulting often ends at advice. The sprint is built to move from diagnosis into shipped assets and a usable handoff.

Vs Full Product Build

Product work is heavier, slower, and often unnecessary this early. The sprint fixes the revenue leak before the buyer commits to something larger.

Why The Sprint Wins

Focus, speed, and buying clarity matter more than category labels.

Clearer To Buy

One bottleneck, one package, one price range. That is easier to approve than broad undefined service work.

Faster To Ship

The narrow scope removes drag. That is why the delivery window can stay short without feeling reckless.

Easier To Prove

Because the scope is tight, the before-after story is obvious and easier to trust than a long mixed project.
