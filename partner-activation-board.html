Partner deal activation page

Once the partner model is chosen, activate the first deal cleanly.

This page is for the moment after a partner says yes to working together. The goal is not to explain the whole model again. The goal is to confirm whether this is referral, white-label, or reseller, confirm who is paying, confirm the commercial logic, move the funds, and start the first partner cycle.

Activation Rule

Partnership is real only after the first live commercial cycle starts.

Good partner activation is simple. Confirm the collaboration model, confirm who controls the client and payment, confirm the payout or margin logic, then collect the transfer and start the first deal. Do not leave the partner in a vague "we should work together" state.
