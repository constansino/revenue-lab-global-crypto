Referral Proof Family Rule

Keep the proof in the referral public proof family unless the public job now clearly belongs to the broader warm-intro proof system.

Referral-safe proof can travel farther without becoming generic. Good routing chooses one proof family first, keeps buyer and referrer identity locked, and stops one approved asset from drifting between referral and original-intro claims without a deliberate reason.
