The real job is choosing which public proof family it should strengthen first: should it help the original warm-intro public proof system keep converting, or should it help a closed buyer introduce one more fit lead through the referral public proof system?

Public Proof Family Rule

Choose the proof family first, then choose the narrower proof board, router, or page inside that family.

One approved proof asset can help more than one commercial path, but it should not do all of them at once. Good routing chooses the proof family first, keeps the hidden buyer and relationship path locked, and prevents the same asset from drifting between original-intro and referral proof claims.
