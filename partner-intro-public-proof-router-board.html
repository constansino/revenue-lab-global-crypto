Best Destinations

Most widened warm-intro proof belongs in one of these six destinations.

Intro-Specific Public Proof Page

Best when the asset still needs warm-intro context and should sit on one narrow public page instead of the wider proof system.

Public Trust Page

Best when the main job is lightweight credibility rather than a bigger reusable proof block.

Proof-Library Slot

Best when the asset should stay reusable, tagged, and compact before any broader public page placement happens.

Broader Proof Board

Best when the proof now supports a wider pattern outside the original intro lane and can travel without hidden context.

Case-Study Route Later

Best when the broader public moment truly needs more narrative than one proof unit can safely carry.

Stay Private Instead

Best when wider public placement adds more exposure than leverage and the proof still works inside repeat lanes or direct conversations.
