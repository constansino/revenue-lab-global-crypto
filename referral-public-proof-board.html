Proof Shapes

These are the proof shapes that usually travel farthest without overexposure.

Compact Result Strip

One line on buyer type, bottleneck, and shift without exposing who the buyer or referrer actually was.

Referral-Safe Proof Block

A compact summary that shows movement while keeping referral mechanics and hidden economics out of the public layer.

Trust-Led Note

One public-safe line that supports credibility on a trust page without becoming a broader narrative.

Proof Page Block

One reusable evidence block that sits on a proof page without immediately widening into a broader public story.

Compact Case Excerpt

One bounded before-after excerpt when the audience needs more narrative than a proof strip can carry.

Do-Not-Widen Block

A referral public proof asset that stays valuable precisely because it does not expand into every page and claim at once.
