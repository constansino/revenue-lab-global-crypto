Best Upgrade Cases

The audit converts best when the problem is obvious and fixable.

Offer Confusion

If the audit shows a weak or muddy offer, the sprint becomes a packaging and close-path fix with clear upside.

Reply Or Intake Friction

If the leak sits in replies or intake, the sprint can produce a more usable message flow, form logic, or handoff process.

Payment-To-Start Drag

If the audit shows buyers stall after agreement, the sprint can tighten payment, kickoff, and start mechanics directly.

Short Upgrade Lines

Use compact language after the audit lands.

Recommendation Line

The audit made the main bottleneck clear. The next useful step is one narrow sprint to fix that exact point instead of more theory.

Value Line

The audit answered what is wrong. The sprint is the step that turns that insight into assets and a cleaner revenue path.

Close Line

If you want me to implement the fix, the clean next move is the sprint deposit and then immediate kickoff.
