Referral Trust Rule

A referral trust page should transfer belief, not the whole private history behind the win.

The public layer should answer five things fast: what changed, why the result is credible, what stays hidden, where the proof belongs first, and what event should trigger a wider public asset later.

Trust Signals

These are the referral signals that usually earn trust without overexposure.

Anonymous Trust Line

One short line on account type, bottleneck, and shift when the result is useful but the buyer must stay hidden.

Brandless Before-After Card

A stripped proof card that shows movement without logos, screenshots, or traceable delivery detail.

Quote-Led Trust Block

One approved quote when voice matters more than volume and the public page only needs one credible sentence.

Compact Proof Note

One proof block that lives on a trust page or proof page without turning into a wider public story.

Process Credibility Signal

One short line on what became clearer, safer, or easier that makes the working model feel believable.

Repeat-Lane Support

A proof unit that mainly helps the same lane repeat, without needing more public surface area.
