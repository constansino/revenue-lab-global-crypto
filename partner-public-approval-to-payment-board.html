Where Public Approvals Leak

Most approval-to-payment stalls in partner public threads come from one of these handoff failures.

No Named Payer

Someone on the buyer side said yes, but nobody named the person who actually opens the wallet and sends.

Approval And Payment Are Split

The commercial yes sits with one person while the operational transfer owner sits with another person who never got the route.

Wallet Friction Is Hidden

The payer may be willing but still uncertain about network, address, exchange steps, or transfer confidence.

Deposit Meaning Is Fuzzy

Payment slows down when the transfer is not tied cleanly to the exact approved first step and the current slot.

Thread Ownership Is Split

Public partner deals lose speed when the buyer contact, approver, and payer each think someone else owns the next move.
