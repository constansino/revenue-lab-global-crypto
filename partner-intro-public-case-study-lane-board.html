Public case-study lane selector

Decide whether one approved public case asset should help the original warm-intro case lane or the referral case lane next.

Once one case-safe asset is approved for public use, the next job is not publishing it across every path. The real job is choosing the case lane it should strengthen first: should it help the original warm-intro case system keep converting, or should it help a closed buyer introduce one more fit lead through the referral case system?

Public Case Lane Rule

Choose the case lane first, then choose the narrower case page, router, or converter destination inside that lane.

One approved case asset can help more than one commercial path, but it should not do all of them at once. Good routing chooses the case lane first, keeps the hidden buyer and relationship path locked, and prevents the same case asset from drifting between original-intro and referral case claims.
