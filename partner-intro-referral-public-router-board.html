Referral public route selector

Decide whether a referral-safe public asset belongs in trust, proof, or case before it widens.

Once a client-referred win is approved for some public use, the next job is not publishing it everywhere. The real job is choosing the narrowest public destination that still helps revenue: a trust page for credibility, a proof route for reusable evidence, or a case route for a fuller public narrative only when the moment truly needs it.

Referral Public Router Rule

Choose the smallest public job first, then route to the narrower referral-safe board that can carry it cleanly.

Referral assets widen best when credibility, proof, and case work stay separate. Good routing makes the public job explicit, keeps the hidden buyer and referrer layer locked, and prevents one approved signal from turning into accidental full-story exposure.
