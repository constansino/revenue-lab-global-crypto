Decide whether one approved partner-safe story asset should stay in the partner public story lane or shift into the referral public story lane next.

Once one partner-safe story asset is already approved for public use, the next job is not cloning it across every page. The real job is choosing the story lane it should strengthen first: should it keep serving broader partner-channel demand, or should it help the referral-public story lane carry one more aligned intro?

Story Lane Rule

Choose the story lane first, then choose the narrower story boundary, router, or case destination inside that lane.

One approved story asset can help more than one commercial path, but it should not do all of them at once. Good routing chooses the story lane first and keeps the hidden source path fixed.
