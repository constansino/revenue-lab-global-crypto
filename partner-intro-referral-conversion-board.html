Referral Conversion Rule

Use the trust of the referral to shorten the deal, not to leave responsibility blurred.

Good client-referral conversion answers five things fast: does the fit still hold, who owns the thread now, which page carries the commercial context, what stays out of the original buyer's lane, and what the next action is if the lead is real.
