Public lane selector

Decide whether one public-safe asset should help the original warm-intro lane or the referral lane next.

Once one approved public asset exists, the next job is not sending it everywhere. The real job is choosing the commercial lane it should strengthen: should it help the original warm-intro lane keep converting, or should it help a closed buyer introduce another fit lead through the referral lane first?

Public Lane Rule

Choose the lane first, then choose the narrower trust, proof, or case job inside that lane.

One public-safe asset can help revenue in more than one way, but it should not do all of them at once. Good routing chooses the lane first, keeps the hidden relationship structure locked, and prevents a public asset from drifting between original-intro and referral claims.
