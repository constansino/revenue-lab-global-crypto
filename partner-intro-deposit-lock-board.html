Do Not Do This

Most deposit-lock attempts fail because they use timing without enough commercial clarity.

Do Not Use It Before Scope Is Clear

If the first step is still fuzzy, tightening the scope matters more than talking about commitment timing.

Do Not Skip Payer Routing

A deal can feel approved and still stall if nobody knows who actually sends the transfer.

Do Not Reopen The Whole Pitch

Deposit lock should sound like an execution step, not a replay of trust, fit, and proof from the start of the thread.

Do Not Force Artificial Urgency

Real urgency comes from delivery bandwidth and timing, not fake countdown language.

Do Not Keep Timing Detached From Payment

The point is to connect current timing to the actual transfer, not just mention urgency in abstract terms.
