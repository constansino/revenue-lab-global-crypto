Public story family selector

Decide whether one approved public-safe story asset should help the original warm-intro story family or the referral story family next.

Once one story-safe asset is approved for public use, the next job is not dropping it into every public narrative. The real job is choosing which public story family it should strengthen first: should it help the original warm-intro public story system keep converting, or should it help a closed buyer introduce one more fit lead through the referral public story system?

Public Story Family Rule

Choose the story family first, then choose the narrower story boundary, public router, or case path inside that family.

One approved story asset can help more than one commercial path, but it should not do all of them at once. Good routing chooses the story family first, keeps the hidden buyer and relationship path locked, and prevents the same story asset from drifting between original-intro and referral claims.
