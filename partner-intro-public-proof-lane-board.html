Public proof lane selector

Decide whether one approved public proof asset should help the original warm-intro proof lane or the referral proof lane next.

Once one proof asset is approved for public use, the next job is not cloning it across every page. The real job is choosing the proof lane it should strengthen first: should it help the original warm-intro proof system keep converting, or should it help a closed buyer introduce one more fit lead through the referral proof system?

Public Proof Lane Rule

Choose the proof lane first, then choose the narrower proof page, router, or library destination inside that lane.

One approved proof asset can help more than one commercial path, but it should not do all of them at once. Good routing chooses the proof lane first, keeps the hidden buyer and relationship path locked, and prevents the same asset from drifting between original-intro and referral proof claims.
