Client-referral brandless proof page

Use one brandless proof asset when the shift should travel but every visible trace of the buyer and referrer should disappear.

Brandless proof is stricter than ordinary anonymous proof. The line should still show the before-after shift, but logos, screenshots, product names, URLs, unusual numbers, and referral path clues all stay out. This page keeps that tradeoff deliberate so the proof stays useful without exposing the lane.

Referral Brandless Rule

Brandless referral proof works when the operating shift stays obvious after the identifying context is removed.

Good brandless proof answers five things cleanly: what kind of bottleneck existed, what first step shipped, what changed, which visible clues were stripped, and which referral-path details stay hidden. If the shift disappears with the brand, the proof is too weak. If the brand is still inferable, the proof is not truly brandless.

Best Formats

Most referral brandless proof fits one of these stripped formats.

Before-After Note

One short contrast line about what changed, with the visible context removed and the operating difference preserved.

Generalized Summary

A small proof block with distinctive nouns, links, screenshots, and branded references generalized or removed.

Workflow Contrast

Describe the shift in process quality rather than the identity of the team, page, or internal setup.

UI-Free Result Line

Keep interface and visual artifacts out so the asset cannot be tied back to a recognizable product or account.

Approver-Safe Summary

Keep the wording plain enough for an operator or approver to understand fast without needing private context.

Forwardable Brandless Asset

Keep it short enough to live inside a forward, pack, or repeat lane without reopening the whole story.

What To Strip

These details are usually what turns “anonymous” into “recognizable.”

Logos And Brand Names

Remove visible naming, product marks, company references, and partner-specific labels unless explicitly cleared.

Screenshots And UI Details

Screenshots, panel names, and layout clues often reveal more than expected even when the text itself looks safe.

URLs And Page Names

Exact paths, landing page titles, or flow names can make the account traceable even without explicit brand use.

Unique Numbers

Unusual volumes, dates, or sequence markers can become identity clues if the lane is small or distinctive.

Stack And Internal Mechanics

Tooling, markup logic, and internal workflow details usually do not belong in the proof asset.

Referral Path Clues

Keep hidden how the intro happened, who connected it, and how the buyer path moved behind the scenes.
