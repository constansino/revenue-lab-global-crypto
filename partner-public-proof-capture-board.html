What Is Usually Safe

Most partner-safe proof capture should stay inside these boundaries.

Safe To Reuse

The bottleneck, the type of fix, one short outcome line, and one anonymous description of the account pattern.

Sometimes Safe

A short partner quote, one sanitized delivery summary, or one bounded case angle with the brand removed.

Usually Not Safe

Client brand names, private economics, internal markup logic, screenshots, and the hidden partner operating path.

Best Default

Start with one short anonymous win summary unless the partner explicitly approves something wider.

Short Ask Lines

Use proof asks the partner can approve quickly.

Quote Ask

If useful, one short line on what changed and why this cycle felt worth repeating would already be enough.

Anonymous Ask

If naming is sensitive, I can keep this fully anonymous and only reference the team type, bottleneck, and visible shift.

Boundary Ask

The easiest version is to confirm what is safe to mention and what must stay hidden before anything gets reused publicly.

Forwardable Ask

I can turn this into one short line you can resend the next time a similar account appears.
