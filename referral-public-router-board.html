Referral Public Router Rule

Choose the smallest public job first, then route to the narrower board that can carry it cleanly.

Referral-safe assets widen best when credibility, proof, and case work stay separate. Good routing makes the public job explicit, keeps the hidden buyer and referrer layer locked, and prevents one approved signal from turning into accidental full-story exposure.
