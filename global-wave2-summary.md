# Global Wave 2 Summary

第二波主要盯的是：

- boilerplate
- starter kit
- onboarding / conversion tools
- builder-facing SaaS products

这些目标的共同点是：

- 页面通常已经能卖
- 但 warm traffic 的承接和下一步动作还不够强
- 很适合从 offer clarity、capture、follow-up 角度切入

## Wave 2 Targets

### 1. Bullet Launch

- URL: https://www.bulletlaunch.com/
- 审站结论: no visible web form for lead capture; no obvious phone CTA above the fold
- 切入角度: 强化 warm traffic capture 和下一步引导

### 2. LaunchSaaS

- URL: https://launchsaas.org/
- 审站结论: no visible web form for lead capture; no obvious phone CTA above the fold
- 切入角度: 把模板流量更好导向注册、咨询或成交动作

### 3. HackerStartup

- URL: https://hackerstartup.com/
- 审站结论: no visible web form for lead capture; no obvious phone CTA above the fold
- 切入角度: 从 landing-to-next-step 的承接逻辑切入

### 4. StartSaaS

- URL: https://startsaas.io/
- 审站结论: no visible web form for lead capture; no clear scheduling or booking step
- 切入角度: 强化 pricing / CTA 后的动作承接

### 5. Omnikit

- URL: https://www.omnikit.dev/
- 审站结论: no visible web form for lead capture; no obvious phone CTA above the fold
- 切入角度: 把感兴趣访问者更快推进到可执行下一步

### 6. SaaS Starter

- URL: https://saasstarter.work/
- 审站结论: no visible web form for lead capture; no obvious phone CTA above the fold
- 切入角度: 先从高意向流量的 capture 和 follow-up 说起

### 7. Wally

- URL: https://www.trywally.ai/
- 审站结论: no visible web form for lead capture; no obvious phone CTA above the fold
- 切入角度: 从 conversion lift 和 faster response layer 切入

## 建议优先级

优先先打这 3 个：

1. Wally
2. Bullet Launch
3. StartSaaS

原因：

- 产品定位比较清楚
- 更容易从 conversion 或 next-step clarity 切入
- 你的 offer 更容易被对方快速理解
