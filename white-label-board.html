Delivery Logic

White-label works when scope, communication, and handoff stay clean.

Defined Scope

Keep the sprint narrow enough that the partner can sell it consistently and the delivery does not turn into custom sprawl.

Single Contact Path

Decide whether communication stays fully through the partner or whether there is a limited shared channel for faster handoff.

Usable Handoff

The output should leave the partner with pages, scripts, or process assets they can keep using after delivery.

Commercial Model

Do not custom-price every partner deal from scratch.

Defined Delivery Rate

Use a known internal delivery rate per sprint so the partner can add their own markup without rebuilding the math each time.

Partner Margin

The partner keeps the spread they add on top of the delivery price, instead of negotiating a new revenue share for every case.
