Once a partner-safe win is cleared for public case use, the next job is not publishing it everywhere. The real job is routing it: should it stay on one narrow case page, move into the next converter for revenue follow-through, shrink back into tighter proof or trust formats, or support referral reuse without exposing the private channel path?

Case Router Rule

Promote one approved case-safe asset into one clearer public job without turning it into a loose permission slip.

A partner-safe case asset can widen commercially without widening narratively. Good routing keeps the spine narrow, the next ask explicit, and the converter jump separate from broader publication drift.
