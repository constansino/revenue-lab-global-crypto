Ready-To-Send Lines

Keep the balance ask calm and specific.

Standard Balance Ask

The agreed sprint assets are now delivered. If everything looks good, the next step is the remaining balance, then I will finalize the handoff package.

With Light Revision

If there is one light revision inside the original scope, send it now and I will tighten it before final handoff.

Short Version

Delivered and ready for approval. If it looks good, send the remaining balance and I will close out the sprint cleanly.

Scope Boundary

Do not let balance collection reopen the sprint.

Light Revision

Small tightening on copy, CTA wording, or structural clarity is a normal close-out step if it stays inside the original sprint.

New Scope

New flows, extra pages, or another bottleneck belong in a new sprint and should not block the current balance collection.
