Referral Story Family Rule

Keep the story in the referral public story family unless the public narrative job now clearly belongs to the broader warm-intro story system.

Referral-safe story can travel farther without becoming generic. Good routing chooses one story family first, keeps buyer and referrer identity locked, and stops one approved asset from drifting between referral and original-intro claims without a deliberate reason.
