Once a partner-safe quote, proof line, or case-study angle is approved for public use, the next job is not publishing it everywhere. The real job is choosing the smallest public destination that still helps revenue: trust for credibility, proof for reusable evidence, or case study for a fuller public narrative only when the moment truly needs it.

Partner Public Router Rule

Choose the smallest public job first, then route to the narrower board that can carry it cleanly.

Partner-channel assets widen best when credibility, proof, and case work stay separate. Good routing makes the public job explicit, keeps the hidden relationship layer locked, and prevents one approved signal from turning into accidental full-story exposure.
