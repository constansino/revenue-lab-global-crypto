Planner Rule

Approvers, signers, and CC viewers should not discover the workflow in production.

Public repo issues keep showing the same problems: approvals do not block signatures, CC recipients do not see the right state, and signers do not receive the expected completion flow. This page turns that into a practical planning tool.

Inputs
approvers, signers, viewers, reminders, delivery
Output
phase order, notification rules, brief
Use
pre-sale planning or internal workflow alignment
