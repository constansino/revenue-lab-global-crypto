Public Lane Rule

Choose the lane first, then choose the narrower trust, proof, or case job inside that lane.

One public-safe asset can help revenue in more than one way, but it should not do all of them at once. Good routing chooses the lane first, keeps the hidden relationship structure locked, and prevents a public asset from drifting between partner and referral claims.
