Intro Family Rule

Choose the intro family first, then choose the narrower intro board, public family, or forward route inside that family.

One approved intro asset can help more than one commercial path, but it should not do all of them at once. Good routing chooses the intro family first, keeps the hidden relationship path locked, and prevents the same asset from drifting between original-intro and referral claims.
