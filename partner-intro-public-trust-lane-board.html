Public Trust Lane Rule

Choose the trust lane first, then choose the narrower trust page, router, or proof escalation inside that lane.

One approved trust asset can help more than one commercial path, but it should not do all of them at once. Good routing chooses the trust lane first, keeps the hidden buyer and relationship path locked, and prevents the same credibility asset from drifting between original-intro and referral trust claims.
